The Clinical Process

Our goal is to provide cost effective medical care, save time, and make you feel better sooner. We will only treat you online if we thinks it is safe.

Step 1

Answer some online questions regarding your symptoms. This will help us decide if you can be treated online or if you should visit a doctor's office.

Step 2

If we can treat your medical problem, you'll create an secure account and enter payment information.

Step 3

Your answers are reviewed by a board-certified doctor. You will be emailed a treatment plan and any medication(s) will be electronically sent to the pharmacy (of your choice).

Step 4

If needed, a follow up is free of charge. We can reply via email, text, or a call back.
